    search engines

    waybackmachine: search the internets archives and deleted comments, pages, posts and history

    whatsmyname: search usernames and find all the sites the name is used for

    webcrawler: meta search engine

    paste

    doxbin: find leaked data

    social

    redit

    redditmetis: search reddit accounts and and get an analysis on a reddit account

    snapchat

    blackmoji: snapchat bitmoji url searcher with bitmoji id, see users bitmoji history

    breaches

    breachdirectory: check of an email or username has been compromised

    leakpeek: search for email, username, passwords, domains, ip addresses and more for breaches

    intelligencex: data breach search engine to search for domain, url, emails and more for breaches

    shadow libaries

    annasarchives: search public archives

    ddosecrets: articles and database of hacked and leaked documents in the public interest

    wikileak: published documents of political and historical importance that are censored and suppressed
